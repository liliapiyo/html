My name is Lilian M. Achieng Apiyo, from Kano Kabonyo. I am the fourth born in a family of ten. I am married in Migori Suna - wanje to my beloved husband Mr. Erich Odongo. I studied at Arombo Primary School and obtained 386 marks out of 700 marks in my K.C.P.E. Thereafter i proceeded to Mawego Girls High School in Homa Bay County and sat for my K.C.S.E and got a mean grade of B-. I then joined JKUAT to study Bsc. in Information Technology and Management. After my graduation, i worked in the IT field for three years before proceeding to Kenya Technical Teachers' Training College in Nairobi to study education in Information Technology. Immediately thereafter, i was employed by T.S.C at Ramogi Institute of Advaced Technology in ICT department to teach computer studies units. During my service as a teacher, I joined The Technical University of Kenya to study Btech. in Computing and information technology to enhance my skills in computing.
